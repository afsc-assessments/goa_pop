Model Symbols and Definitions

Symbol Definition
( y ) Year
( a ) Age class
( a_0 ) Age at recruitment (age-2)
( a_+ ) Plus group age (age-31 and older)
( s ) Sex (male or female)
( f ) Fleet (fixed (LL), trawl (TR))
( _s^{} ) Logistic slope for fixed-gear selectivity (sex ( s ))
( a_{50,s} ) Age at 50% selectivity for fixed gear (sex ( s ))

\(a\) & Age class \(a_0\) & Age at recruitment (age-2) \(a_+\) & Plus group age (age-31 and older) \(s\) & Sex (male or female) \(f\) & Fleet (fixed (LL), trawl (TR)) $s^{log}} & Logistic slope for fixed-gear selectivity (sex \(s\)) $a{50,s} & Age at 50% selectivity for fixed gear (sex s) \(\delta_s^{dome}\) & Dome width parameter for trawl selectivity (sex s) \(a_{\max,s}\) & Age at peak selectivity for trawl gear (sex s) \(p\) & Gamma spread parameter for dome-shaped trawl selectivity \(R_y\) & Annual recruitment at age-2 \(N_{y,a,s}\) & Numbers-at-age in year y , age a, sex s \(Z_{y,a,s}\) & Total instantaneous mortality \(F_{y,a,s,f}\) & Fleet-specific fishing mortality \(F_{y,a,s}\) & Total fishing mortality across fleets \(M_{y,a,s}\) & Natural mortality \(v_{y,a,s,f}\) & Selectivity-at-age by fleet \(r_{y,a,s,f}\) & Retention-at-age by fleet \(d_{y,a,s,f}\) & Discard mortality rate $w_{y,a,s}4 & Weight-at-age $m_{y,a,s}4 & Maturity-at-age \(C_{y,a,f}\) & Catch-at-age by fleet f \(C_y\) & Total catch in year y \(B_y\) & Total biomass in year y \(SSB_y\) & Female spawning stock biomass in year y \(q_{LL}, q_{TR}\) & Survey catchability coefficients \(I_{y,LL}\), I_{y,TR}$ & Survey indices (longline, trawl) \(\sigma_{LL}, \sigma_{TR}\) & Survey index standard deviations \(\epsilon_y\) & Survey observation error \(n^*\) & Sample size for compositions \(P_{a,s}\) & Composition Number of individuals sampled at age

Population dynamics \[ N_{y,a,s} = \begin{cases} R_y/2, & a = a_0 \\ N_{y-1,a-1,s} e^{-Z_{y-1,a-1,s}}, & a_0 < a < a_+ \\ N_{y-1,a-1,s} e^{-Z_{y-1,a-1,s}} + N_{y-1,a_+,s} e^{-Z_{y-1,a_+,s}}, & a = a_+ \end{cases} \]

\[ Z_{y,a,s} = F_{y,a,s} + M_{y,a,s} \]

\[ F_{y,a,s} = \sum_f F_{y,a,s,f} \]

\[ F_{y,a,s,f} = v_{y,a,s,f} \cdot \left[F_{y,f} \cdot r_{y,a,s,f} + d_{y,a,s,f} \cdot (1 - r_{y,a,s,f}) \right] \]

Selectivity Functions
Fixed Gear (Logistic) \[ v_{y,a,s,\text{LL}} = \frac{1}{1 + \exp\left( -\delta_s^{\text{log}} (a - a_{50,s}) \right)} \]

Trawl Gear (Dome-shaped Gamma) \[ v_{y,a,s,\text{TR}} = \left(\frac{a}{a_{+,s}}\right)^{a_{+,s}/p} \cdot \exp\left( \frac{a_{+,s} - a}{p} \right) \quad\text{with } p = 0.5 \sqrt{a_{+,s}^2 + 4\left(\delta_s^{\text{dome}}\right)^2} - a_{+,s} \]

Equations describing observation data

\[ C_{y,a,f} = \sum_{s=1}^2 \frac{F_{y,a,s,f}}{F_{y,a,s,f} + M_{y,a,s}} N_{y,a,s} \left( 1 - e^{-Z_{y,a,s}} \right) w_{y,a,s} \]

Annual Catch \[ C_y = \sum_a \sum_f C_{y,a,f} \]

Annual total biomass
\[ B_y = \sum_a \sum_s N_{y,a,s} w_{y,a,s} \]

Annual spawning stock biomass (SSB)
\[ SSB_y = \sum_a N_{y,a,\text{female}} \cdot w_{y,a,\text{female}} \cdot m_{y,a,\text{female}} \]

Longline Survey (Abundance Index)
\[ I_{y,LL} = q_{LL} \left[ \sum_a \sum_s N_{y,a,s} \cdot e^{-Z_{y,a,s}/2} \cdot v_{y,a,s,LL} \right] \cdot e^{\epsilon_y - \sigma_{LL}^2 / 2} \]

Bottom Trawl Survey (Biomass Index)
\[ I_{y,TR} = q_{TR} \left[ \sum_a \sum_s N_{y,a,s} \cdot e^{-Z_{y,a,s}/2} \cdot v_{y,a,s,BT} \cdot w_{y,a,s} \right] \cdot e^{\epsilon_y - \sigma_{TR}^2 / 2} \]

Age Composition Data
Multinomial Sampling

\[ P_{a,s} \sim \text{Multinomial} \left( \frac{N_{a,s}}{\sum_{a,s} N_{a,s}}, n^* \right) \]

Sample sizes:

  • Longline survey and fixed gear fleet: \(n^* = 50\)
  • Trawl survey and trawl fleet: \(n^* = 30\)
